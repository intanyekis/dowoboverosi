Mark Jindrak Responds To Ruthless Aggression Showing That Triple H Didnt Want Him In Evolut

September 06, 2024
blog

WWE recently released a brand new episode of its show Ruthless Aggression, in which Triple H discusses Mark Jindrak and how he was supposed to be in Evolution. Now, Jindrak has taken to social media to respond.

In the brief clip, Triple H acknowledges that he didn’t want Jindrak in Evolution, and went as far as speaking with Vince McMahon about it. In the end, they did film Evolution promos that included Jindrak, but obviously ended up excluding him from the group in favor for Batista.

After seeing the clip, in which, Jindrak took to Twitter, where he said that it felt “nice” to have closure after so long. According to Jindrak, he understands why Triple H didn’t want him in Evolution, but that he’s still challenging him to a match at WrestleMania.
