Since its launch in the early 2000s, Keeping Up With The Kardashians quickly became one of the most popular reality TV shows of all time, with fans tuning in on a regular basis to capture a slice of the family drama.

Two family members that many fans have come to adore are Kendall and Kylie, whom many fans have watched grow up on screen together. When the show first started, the two girls were still considerably young, with Kendall being eleven years old and Kylie just nine years old.

They are both now in their twenties, and it's fair to say they have both grown up a lot since the show first aired. During their time on-screen, the two girls have been connected to various romances, with some lasting longer than others.

Who Is Kendall Jenner's Current Boyfriend?

Kendall Jenner is officially dating Devin Booker, an American NBA star who plays for the Phoenix Suns of the National Basketball Association. The 25-year-old has been spotted on Kendall's Instagram on numerous occasions, where the pair flaunt their super cute dates together.

However, some fans actually don't think that the young pair share any romantic chemistry at all, and weren't afraid to share their thoughts in the comments, prompting Kendall to delete the pictures shortly after. Despite some initial 'hate', Kendall has still continued to share photos of her and her beau.

However, Devin Booker isn't the only male that Kendall has been romantically involved with. Her previous dating history includes A$AP Rocky, Nick Jonas, Harry Styles, Jordan Clarkson, Blake Griffin, and Ben Simmons, with rumors even flying that she had a short relationship with Justin Bieber.

Is Kendall Jenner Jealous Of Ex A$AP Rocky's Relationship With Rihanna?

Rihanna and A$AP Rocky have recently welcomed a baby into the world together. From 2016-2017, Kendall Jenner also dated A$AP Rocky, however, the two reportedly split due to both of them having difficult schedules.

Still, the recent baby news has fans recalling Rocky's past relationship with Kendall, and whether she's feeling some type of way about his new life.

Whilst Kendall seems unbothered by Rihanna and A$AP, it also seems that she doesn't feel any desire yet to have children, and it seems the reality TV star even feels pressured to do so.

In a recent episode of The Kardashians, a scene between Kendall and her mom, Kris Jenner, shows the two discussing children in her office. Kendall goes states that it's 'her life' and she doesn't know if she's ready yet and still has a lot she needs to figure out before she can welcome a child into her life.
