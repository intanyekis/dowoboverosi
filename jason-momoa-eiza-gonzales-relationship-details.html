The Truth About Jason Momoa And Eiza Gonzlez's Relationship

In January 2022, Jason Momoa surprised fans when he and Lisa Bonet announced that they were getting divorced after 17 years of being together. They have two kids, Lola and Nakoa-Wolf.

"We have all felt the squeeze and changes of these transformational times … A revolution is unfolding ~and our family is of no exception … feeling and growing from the seismic shifts occurring. And so~ We share our Family news~ That we are parting ways in marriage," the couple said in a joint statement.

They continued: "We share this not because we think it’s newsworthy ~ But so that~ as we go about our lives we may do so with dignity and honesty. The love between us carries on, evolving in ways it wishes to be known and lived. We free each other ~to be who we are learning to become … Our devotion unwavering to this sacred life & our Children Teaching our Children What’s possible ~ Living the Prayer May Love Prevail J & L."

A few months later, the Aquaman star was rumored to be "rebounding" with Eiza González. Though the romance was reportedly cut short, they were recently seen together again. Here's everything you should know about their relationship.

How Did Jason Momoa & Eiza Gonzáles Meet?

In May 2022, a source told Us Weekly that Momoa was "dating" González. "It’s very casual," said the insider. "But they are having fun and getting to know each other. Ever since they met, there has been an attraction. Both of them are very attracted to each other and they’re seeing where things go."

That same month, the Game of Thrones star slammed the rumors that he was dating Kate Beckinsale. They had been photographed together at the Vanity Fair Oscars afterparty where he was seen giving her his jacket.

"It was cray. [I] have a conversation with a woman about her country, I was in England doing Aquaman 2. Everyone is like, 'Are you dating?'" the actor said about his encounter with Beckinsale. "No, no, it was chivalry. The woman was cold. … She is very nice. I was being very nice, just being a gentleman. Now I am not giving my coat to anyone."

Meanwhile, González has previously been linked to other actors like Liam Hemsworth, Josh Duhamel, and Timothée Chalamet. In June 2020, the Baby Driver star was spotted with Chalamet while on a trip to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico. She had also been seen with Jeremy Renner at a pre-Super Bowl party in February that year. The Marvel star was said to be "pretty flirtatious" with the Fast & Furious Presents: Hobbs & Shaw actress.

Did Jason Momoa And Eiza González Break Up?

In June 2022, news broke that Momoa and González had broken up. Multiple sources told People that the two were just incompatible. "They're just very different people," said one source. Another added that they were "hoping they might work it out" and that "they love each other very much having dated quietly for some time before it became public.

They are in different life stages." Gonzáles is 10 years younger than Momoa — another issue outlets were fixated on when they started dating.

But not long before their rumored split, a source said that the pair was actually a good match. Gonzáles was described as this "very fun, down-for-anything type person and is pretty adventurous for the most part" and that she and Momoa "share that same spirit." They added that "she's more of a long-term-relationship person versus casual dater while he is "a really good guy, very generous and happy all the time; he's all about love and spreading love."

Are Jason Momoa & Eiza González Still Together?

Shortly after their rumored breakup, the couple was still spotted together in London numerous times in July. Recently, they were also photographed having a "romantic motorcycle ride" in Malibu together.

Though their romance was initially described as "casual," a source later clarified that Momoa was simply taking things slow for the sake of his kids. "It's not that he's not taking it seriously, but with his kids, he's not going to rush into anything quickly," said the insider.

"He had a lot of love for Lisa and there is still a lot of mutual respect there as far as she's concerned," the source continued. Who knows, the couple might make their red carpet debut soon. They were just photographed attending González's Michael Bay film, Ambulance back in April. In the meantime, Momoa maintains a co-parenting relationship with Bonet. In March, the actor said that his ex-wife and his ex-stepdaughter Zoë Kravitz are "still family."
