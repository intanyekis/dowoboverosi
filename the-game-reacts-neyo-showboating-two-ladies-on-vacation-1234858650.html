The Game Reacts To Ne-Yo Showboating His Two Ladies On Vacation

June 09, 2024
blog

The Game and nearly all of social media has had something to say about Ne-Yo parading around Jamaica with two women recently. In footage posted to a woman named Arielle Hill‘s Instagram on Wednesday (Mar. 6), the “So Sick” crooner could be seen having the time of his life in a perceived throuple.

One clip showed the trio blowing kisses to the camera as both ladies cuddle up to 44-year-old. Another post showed both ladies posing on a car-shaped jet ski and standing on the back of his 4-wheeler.

“I said what I said …You heard what YOU heard …YOU saw what YOU saw and I did what did and will continue to do what I do,” Arielle captioned clips of her, Ne-Yo and the unnamed woman. “You thought what YOU thought …but REGARDLESS it is what it is.” Ne-Yo commented, “Damn right TF, damn right PB… Damn right, 6Eva.”

Social media quickly caught wind and began giving its two cents, including The Game, who seemed to be all for the trio under The Shade Room‘s repost of the footage.

“They both look like they say ‘spit in my mouth,’” he commented. “I’m here for it [crying face emoji].”

Although the R&B sensation’s actions may be unfavorable by some, he is a single man. Ne-Yo finalized his divorce to ex-wife, Crystal Renay Smith, in February of 2023 after claims that he “cheated multiple times” during their eight-year marriage and “secretly had a child” with another woman.

“8 years of lies and deception. 8 years of unknowingly sharing my life and husband with numerous of women who sell their bodies to him unprotected… every last one of them,” she said in an Instagram post at the time of separation. “To say I’m heartbroken and disgusted is an understatement. To ask me to stay and accept it is absolutely insane. The mentality of a narcissist. I will no longer lie to the public or pretend that this is something it isn’t. I choose me, I choose my happiness and health and my respect.”

Ne-Yo responded to the allegations via Twitter, requesting that people respect his family’s privacy as they work through their issues.

Additionally Ne-Yo’s ex-fiancée, Monyetta Shaw, called off their engagement in 2013 due to claims that Ne-Yo had an excessive desire for threesomes. In an interview with Haus of Aaron, the Real Housewives of Atlanta star said she and Ne-Yo “cheated together” throughout their relationship by welcoming outsiders into their bedroom.

“He wasn’t ready,” she said. “We cheated together if that makes sense and then it just wasn’t enough and I was like, ‘Well, no. I didn’t sign up to do this every day. [I’m] just very multifaceted and that’s where I was at that time, but it became a problem when you want that, you know, a lot. Like, maybe I’ll give you that maybe twice a year…but it was too much.”
