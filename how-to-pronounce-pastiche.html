What does the word pastiche mean?

A pastiche is a work of visual art, literature, theatre, or music that imitates the style or character of the work of one or more other artists. The word pastiche is a French cognate of the Italian noun pasticcio, which is a pâté or pie-filling mixed from diverse ingredients.

Is pastiche an insult?

The All-Purpose Insult: PASTICHE!

Pastiche is defined as “a literary, artistic, musical, or architectural work that imitates the style of previous work.” The key word is “imitates.” Most artistic endeavor is influenced by—but does not directly copy—what has gone before.
