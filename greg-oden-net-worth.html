What is Greg Oden’s Net Worth and Salary?

Greg Oden, a former professional basketball player, has a net worth of $14 million. He played for the Portland Trail Blazers and the Miami Heat but faced a challenging career due to frequent injuries.

Early Life and Basketball Career

Greg Oden was born on January 22, 1988, in Buffalo, New York, and later moved to Terra Haute, Indiana, where he attended Sarah Scott Middle School. He then relocated to Indianapolis, Indiana, and played basketball for Lawrence North High School, leading them to three consecutive Indiana Class 4A basketball championships. Oden was named the National Boys Basketball Player of the Year in 2005 and 2006.

In 2006, Oden attended Ohio State University, where he missed the first half of the season due to a wrist injury. He made his college debut on December 2, 2006, finishing the game with 14 points, ten rebounds, and five blocks. Oden was described as a “once-in-a-decade player” by sports broadcaster Steve Kerr and earned Defensive Player of the Year and All-American honors in 2007.

After one year of college basketball, Oden announced that he would be entering the 2007 NBA draft.

Greg Oden’s Career and Current Role

Injuries plagued Greg Oden’s NBA career, causing him to miss multiple seasons and limiting his playing time. Despite setbacks, Oden made a comeback with the Miami Heat in 2014, but was unable to continue his NBA career after that. In 2015, he signed with the Jiangsu Dragons of the Chinese Basketball Association but left the team mid-season. Today, Oden works as an athlete adviser for Edyoucore Sports & Entertainment, providing financial education and consulting services to amateur and professional athletes.

Greg Oden’s NBA Salary and Endorsements
