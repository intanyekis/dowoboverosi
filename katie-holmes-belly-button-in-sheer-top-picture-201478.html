Katie Holmes tried a different spin on the crop top trend at The Lexus Short Films premiere on Aug. 6 in NYC. The Giver star, 35, wore a one-shouldered Michael Kors top that featured a sheer panel on the midriff, all the better to showcase her belly button. The shirt also had a ruffle detail along the bust and a voluminous bell sleeve.

The mom to daughter Suri, 8, paired the chic top with black pants and paisley "Avra" pumps, all by Michael Kors.

With her hair swept up into a messy top knot, Holmes, a spokeswoman for Bobbi Brown, played up her eyes with black liner and highlighted her radiant complexion with pink blush and a rose lipstick.
