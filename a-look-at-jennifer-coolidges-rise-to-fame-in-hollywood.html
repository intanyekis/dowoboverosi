A Look At Jennifer Coolidge's Rise To Fame In Hollywood

She’s recently gained critical acclaim for her roles in the Netflix short series The Watcher and HBO Max’ The White Lotus, however, actress Jennifer Coolidge has been landing roles in film and television for decades.

Delivering a supporting role in everything from episodes of Seinfeld to playing Stifler’s mom in the American Pie franchise, Coolidge always brought a unique look, drama and a contagious comedic flair to her roles.

She may have a net worth of $6 million today, but fans would bet that’s set to increase exponentially as Coolidge has officially arrived as a frontrunner in popular shows; starring as the lead character, lending her voice to animated films and raking in the awards for her performances.

Check out how Coolidge has progressed over the course of 50 years or more.

The Early Years Of Coolidge

The 61-year-old actress was born in Boston and at one time, thought she may play the clarinet professionally. Her love for the orchestra was a natural springboard into the drama programs in high school. She attended the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in New York City and according to Celebrity Net Worth, Coolidge planned on becoming a dramatic actress, frequently referencing Meryl Streep as her inspiration. After finding her love for comedy and receiving positive feedback for her natural comedic timing, she made a switch to playing the funny role we all know and love.

Landing Small Roles

Coolidge started out small, with roles in an episode of Seinfeld and a small role in the film A Night at the Roxbury, starring comedians Will Ferrell and Chris Kattan. In those early years, the actress worked at a bar in New York with up-and-coming star Sandra Bullock. While Bullock’s career took off, Coolidge was repeatedly told she wasn’t pretty enough but all of that changed when she landed the role she’s been known for over the years.

Her Big Break

In 1999, Coolidge played Jeanine Stifler, better known as Stifler’s mom in the hit comedy franchise American Pie. Playing a seductive older woman to a group of teens, Coolidge gained popularity with her wit and good looks. She reprised her role in the franchise in 2001 and again in 2003 for American Wedding. Just two years later, Coolidge landed a supporting role opposite Reese Witherspoon in Legally Blonde to huge success at the box office and she returned to the role in Legally Blond 2: Red, White & Blonde.

During the height of her film success, Coolidge also starred in 2004’s A Cinderella Story opposite Hillary Duff and the unique Lemony Snicket novel to television series, A Series of Unfortunate Events.

Becoming A Familiar Voice

Coolidge has a distinctive voice and dramatic tone, in which animated studios latched on to quickly. In the mid-1990’s she had already lent her voice to the adult animated show, King of the Hill. As her career soared, fans could recognize that unique voice in feature films like Robots and Aunt Fanny’s Tour of Booty, a follow up short to the Blue Sky Studios hit. In the last few years, we’ve heard Coolidge behind characters in The Fungies, Rick and Morty, The Emoji Movie and Fish Hooks to name a few.

Finally Achieveing Stardom

To date, Coolidge has found her success in films and television shows ranging from Adam Sandler’s Click to Beauty & the Briefcase with frequent co-star, Hillary Duff. Most recently, the star has gained notice for her work on Netflix’s The Watcher alongside Naomi Watts and Mia Farrow as well as her turn as Tanya McQuoid in The White Lotus as a self-absorbed woman whose mother recently died and she’s seeking inner peace. Also in 2023, Coolidge will star in the Netflix comedy, We Have a Ghost and alongside Jennifer Lopez and Josh Duhamel in Shotgun Wedding.

Awards And Recognition

It was her turn in The White Lotus that earned Coolidge a Golden Globe and Primetime Emmy Award for Best Supporting Actress in Television. In addition, the actress received a Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Female Performance. In 2023, Coolidge was cited as being one of TIME Magazine’s Most Influential People. According to INSIDER, MTV has announced Coolidge will be the recipient of their 2023 comedic genius award; only granted to few others in the award show’s history.

Giving It Back

Coolidge is known for her charity work, specifically related to animal rights. In fact, just this year, the animal lover was recognized by PETA for her commitment to animal liberation. She was crowned ‘Vegan Queen” this past March. In addition, she starred in a 2023 Super Bowl ad for vegan brand, e.l.f. beauty. She’s also supported causes related to AIDS assistance among others.

A Movie-Set-Like Mansion

According to IMDB, the comedian owns two homes: one in Hollywood and the other in New Orleans. Prior to Hurricane Katrina, Coolidge purchased a historic mansion in the area and then became involved in the city restoration after tragedy struck. Her mansion has also served as a filming location. The 2017, Sofia Coppola civil war drama, The Beguiled was filmed there. The $10 million home has been considered haunted and includes interior décor like dark oil paintings on the walls, warm candle lit hallways, paired with a scary annual Coolidge Halloween party.
