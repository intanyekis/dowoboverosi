Will Urban Meyer Be Arrested For Kicking? Josh Lambo Allegations Explained

June 30, 2024
blog

There is no confirmation if Urban Meyer will be arrested for kicking Josh Lambo. However, the coach has been fired by the Jaguars. 

Urban Meyer is an American Football coach. He had been a head coach for different teams, including Bowling Green Falcons, the Utah Utes, the Florida Gators, and the Ohio State Buckeyes. 

In 2021, he had retired from coaching but later came out of retirement and took the position and head coach in Jacksonville Jaguars. Nonetheless, different controversies surrounded him after joining the team, which ultimately led to him getting fired. 

Will Urban Meyer Be Arrested For Kicking?

Urban Meyer has been accused of kicking Josh Lambo, but no arrest has been made yet. Josh, the kicker for the team, has said that his coach physically assaulted him during practice. 

The Guardian wrote that the player told a Florida newspaper that his coach kicked him during August practice. The controversy surged, which eventually resulted in Meyer getting kicked out from his first job in NFL.

The coach who got success in every step in college football simply could not make proper adjustments to the level where he is coaching a team of professional players. The first scandal began when he stayed back with his family instead of going with his team during Cincinnati in late September. 

However, the issue started when he was caught inappropriately behaving with a woman in a bar in Ohio at that time. Shad Khan warned him and said that he needed to earn the respect of the owners. But, he failed to do that, and now the team decided to let him go. 

Josh Lambo Allegations On Urban Meyer Explained

Josh Lambo made an allegation on Urban Meyer about kicking him during practice. He detailed the incident to the Tampa Bay Times.
