What Happened Between Zac Brown and Luke Bryan?

During a 2013 radio interview, Zac Brown did not hold back his feelings about Luke Bryan's "My Kind of Night," calling it "the worst song I've ever heard." He went as far as saying the song made him feel nauseous.

This bold critique quickly spread across the internet, provoking extensive discussions and responses from various individuals within the country's music industry.

As the controversy continued to gain momentum, it became clear that the clash between Brown and Bryan was far from over.

Was The Feud Between Zac Brown And Luke Bryan Resolved?
