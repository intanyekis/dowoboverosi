Tom Holland began his training in dramatics from a young age. In 2008, he starred in Billy Elliot, The Musical, critically praised by audiences. Since then, the star worked in minor roles in movies and television shows until he bagged the iconic role of Spider-Man. The world got a glimpse of Spider-Man in Captain America: Civil War in 2016 before his movie Spider-Man: Homecoming hit theaters the very next year. Holland has amassed an $18 million fortune through his projects to become one of the most endearing actors in Hollywood.

A fan of Audi, Tom has a stunning car collection that features not one but four stunning Audis, and his garage also features two new automobiles. Let’s take a look at his crazy car collection.

Audi RS 7 ($114,000)

The German automaker released the second generation of the Audi RS 7 was released in 2020 by the German automaker. The five-seated new iteration got wider by 1.6 inches. With muscular sides, black-out grille, and flared wheel, the RS 7 is the ultimate family sedan. According to Motor Trend, it got equipped with a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 that gives 591 HP and a top speed of 190 MPH. The car was designed as a power-assist hybrid, similar to the A6 and A7 models, and has a 48-volt system. Audi has engineered new acoustics for the interiors that keep the cabin quiet for a long and comfortable journey across town.

Audi Q7 ($55,800)

The three-row Audi that ranked in the second spot of the 2020 Luxury Midsize SUVs, the Q7, is a powerful and futuristic three-row vehicle made for the family. The Q7 is available in three trims: Premium, Premium Plus, and Prestige. On average, it has a 248-500 HP engine with an all-wheel-drive drivetrain. For the 2020 iteration, Audi upgraded the exterior styling and the interior system. It now has two touch screens along with its virtual cockpit that can customize based on the driver’s preference. Additionally, the car has received the highest rating in the safety test category based on six crash tests.

Audi RS 7 Sportback ($118,500)

The sportier version of the RS 7, the Sportback showcases a high performance with a powerful style and a sleek four-door structure that exudes luxury. The RS 7 has introduced a new RS Design Package for the 2022 iteration that will let customers choose the cabin interior, from the accents to the unique floor mats. As mentioned by Car And Driver, it has a twin-turbo powerful V8 paired with a 48-volt hybrid engine to produce 605 HP and 590 lb-ft of torque. The Sportback has robust navigation, a seamless touchscreen infotainment system, and a digital gauge cluster.

Audi R8 ($155,000)

One of the most potent cars the manufacturer has to offer is Audi R8 is, a powerful automobile in its lineup. For the 2021 iteration, the carmaker debuted its rear-wheel-drive for the coupe and the Spyder that gives 532 HP. Also available is the all-wheel drive with a powerful V10 to make 602 HP. The neck-snapping acceleration offers a top speed of 205 MPH and prances to 60 MPH in merely 2.8 seconds. The R8 has a simple layout for the interior, with the instrument cluster also functioning as the infotainment system. The interior textures are available in three shades: brown, gray, and red leather to match the black quilted seats.

Porsche Taycan Turbo S ($185,000)

In October 2021, the actor went big on his purchase and bought a Porsche Taycan Turbo S, the first all-electric car in his collection. Along with some members of his family, the actor went to Stuttgart, Germany, to test the Taycan in its production facility and left the place after purchasing the Porsche. One of its best-performing electric cars, the Turbo S, is a force of nature with a 761 HP, as noted by Porsche. With a top speed of 161 MPH, it reaches 62 MPH in just 2.8 seconds. It offers luxury in the interior and has a spacious infotainment display and the iconic Porsche Connect Voice Pilot for assistance.

Rolls Royce Cullinan Black Badge ($440,000)

Three weeks after purchasing the Porsche, Tom splashed out in November of the same year to bag a Rolls Royce Cullinan Black Badge Edition. Rolls Royce created the first SUV, a powerful piece of machinery produced. Under the hood, it is packed with a 6.75-liter V12 twin-turbo engine that powers through with its eight-speed transmission. The Black Badge generates a staggering 562 HP and 627 lb-ft of maximum torque, as reported by Auto Evolution. The car prances to 62 MPH in five seconds and has a top speed of 155 MPH.

Tom Holland made two big purchases with the Porsche Taycan Turbo S and Rolls Royce Cullinan Black Badge in the last few months. As a fan of speed and luxury, the actor has ensured to adorn his garage with expensive cars.
