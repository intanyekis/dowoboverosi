Hasbulla Disability: What Condition Does Mini Khabib Have?

June 07, 2024
blog

A doctor had posted a video on TikTok claiming that Hasbulla, also known as ‘mini Khabib’, suffers from a rare genetic condition called Growth Hormone deficiency.

It is a disorder that occurs when the pituitary gland in the brain does not produce enough growth hormone, which has led to Hasbulla’s stunted growth and high-pitched voice.

Dr. Raj says that in some cases the condition can be caused by tumors, but a lot of the time doctors are unable to explain exactly why some people with Growth Hormone deficiency have the disorder.

The good news is, if Growth Hormone deficiency is diagnosed in childhood, it can be successfully treated with Human Growth Hormone (HGH) injections to spur development during puberty.

Furthermore, healthy food keeps the HGH production rate to an optimum, by keeping track of your body fat and insulin levels.

To maintain a normal range of human growth hormones in the blood, foods rich in melatonin, such as eggs, fish, mustard seeds, tomatoes, nuts, grapes, and raspberries are highly recommended by experts.
