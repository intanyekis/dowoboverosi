42 Dugg

42 Dugg is the stage name of the American rapper Dion Marquise Hayes. He is born in the year 1995 on 25th November. He was raised in Detroit, Michigan. At the age of 15, he was arrested for carjacking and was initially put behind bars for 4 years which later got extended to 6 years when he picked a fight with his inmate.  During this time in prison, he practised music, wrote lyrics and at the age of 22, he was released. In 2017, he met Lil Baby with whom he later jointly signed along with Yo Gotti in a label 4 Pockets Full and Collective music group with Interscope records. He became famously known for his collaboration with Lil Baby on songs titled “Grace”, “We Paid” which earned him a top ten position in billboard hot 100. The number 42 before his name is a reference to a small clique named ‘Hustle Boys’ where Letters "H"  from Hustle and "B" Boys are represented as numerical digits "4" and "2" on the phone keypad. He is famously known for a technique called mumbled rap and all of his songs kick start with a whistle, which has become his signature style.
